# Some RegEX Tips
| Pattern       | matches                                       | example                   
| :------:      | :------------------------------------:        | :-----------------------: 
| [A-Za-z]+     | upper and lowercase English alphabet          | 'ABCDEFghijk'                               
| [0-9]         | numbers from 0 to 9                           | 9
| [A-Za-z\-\.]+ | upper and lowercase English alphabet, - and . | 'My-Website.com'
| (a-z)         | a, - and z                                    | 'a-z'
| (\s+l,)       | spaces or a comma                             | ', '
